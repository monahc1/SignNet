    How to Use SignNet

    1. Position your hand clearly in front of the camera
    2. For alphabet signs: Hold your hand still in the correct position
    3. For word signs: Perform the complete sign movement
    4. The system will automatically detect whether you're performing a static or dynamic sign

    For best results, ensure good lighting and a clean background.
